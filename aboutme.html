Kimiya Firoozgan

I've been into health and fitness for about eight years now. Over that time, I've tried just about everything, from gym sessions to boutique studios, and I genuinely enjoy taking all of the classes listed on this site and finding a schedule that works best for me. One of my favorite classes we've covered is heated yoga. Beyond being a serious physical challenge, I find it incredibly relaxing and always leave feeling rejuvenated.

My fitness journey started around my senior year of high school when I was trying to gain weight and stumbled into a love for physical activity. What began as a specific goal turned into something much bigger, and now I enjoy going to workout classes with friends and family just as much as I enjoy the workouts themselves. My biggest fitness goal right now is learning how to balance all the classes I love with a busy school and work schedule, while also finding time to learn golf.
